Eh brah! You eva wondah wat da kine dogs do all day at BYU-Hawaii? I tellin' you, it's one good life!

In da morning, dis doggy named "Pua" always wake up early fo' check out da sunrise ova Laie Point. Sunrise time da bes! She wag her tail like crazy, cuz she know da beach is comin' soon.

One student pass by and say, "Howzit Pua, you ready fo' your walk?" Pua jus' bark wit excitement.

On da beach, Pua dig holes an splash inside da wata. She make frens wit everybody—da surfers, da tourists, and da local kids who like play fetch wit her.

Aftah da beach, Pua cruise ova to da campus bookstore fo' say hi to da students buyin' textbooks. Dey always give her small kine treats, especially spam musubi leftovers!

Den latah, Pua run around da campus, smellin' all kine stuffs an checkin' fo' her frens. Sometime, she even find one old sandwich dat somebody wen drop fo'get on da grass. Dat's her special treasure fo da day!

By da Caf, Pua stop an listen to students talkin' story. She always hea stuffs like Harry Potter an BYU-H adventures. he no understand everyting, but da vibes is good.

Next stop fo Pua, da library lanai, wea she take one short nap undah da tables. Students study, an she dream bout da kine chickens she wen chase in da morning—always good fun!

Aftah da nap, Pua make one visit to da hale dorms fo check on her best fren, Kaleo da cat. Dey sit undah da mango tree togetha, watchin' da students go by.
