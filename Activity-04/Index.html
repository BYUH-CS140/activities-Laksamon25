How to Fold a Paper Airplane

Materials | Folding Steps | Tips

Introduction

Folding a paper airplane is easy and fun! Follow these steps to create your own airplane.

Materials Needed

  • 1 sheet of paper
  • A flat surface to fold the paper

Folding Steps

  1. Lay the paper on a flat surface.
  2. Fold the paper in half vertically.
  3. Unfold the paper and fold each of the top corners into the center line.
  4. Fold the top edges again to the center.
  5. Fold the plane in half lengthwise.
  6. Fold the wings down, matching the top edges of the wings with the bottom edge of the body.

Extra Tips

  • Make sure your folds are as sharp as possible.
  • This airplane has stiff wings and can tolerate fast throwing speeds.
  • Throw it as hard as you can for maximum distance and speed.
