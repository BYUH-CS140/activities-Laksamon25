Udon Thani

This stunning temple in Udon Thani, Thailand is Wat Pa Phu Kon (วัดป่าภูก้อน). Located in Na Yung District, this serene forest temple is known for its breathtaking architecture, peaceful surroundings, and its stunning indoor reclining white marble Buddha statue. The photo you shared beautifully captures the temple at night with star trails overhead, showcasing its spiritual atmosphere and natural beauty. It's a popular spot for meditation and photography alike.

The Red Lotus Lake, or Talay Bua Daeng, is one of Udon Thani's most magical and lesser-known natural wonders in northeastern Thailand. Located in Kumphawapi District, this breathtaking lake comes to life between December and February, when millions of pink water lilies bloom across its surface, creating a surreal sea of color as far as the eye can see.
