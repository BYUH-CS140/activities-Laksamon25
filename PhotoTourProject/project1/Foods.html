Foods

Pad Thai is Thailand’s most iconic stir-fried noodle dish, made with rice noodles, shrimp, eggs, bean sprouts, and tofu, all tossed in a savory-sweet tamarind sauce. Topped with crushed peanuts, fresh lime, chili flakes, and herbs, it's a perfect balance of tangy, sweet, and spicy flavors.

Som Tum, or green papaya salad, is a zesty and refreshing Thai dish made from shredded unripe papaya, tomatoes, carrots, chilies, lime, fish sauce, and peanuts. It’s crunchy, spicy, and full of bold, tangy flavor—especially popular in the northeastern Isan region.
